-- Types --
uX = unsigned integer Xbits big endian

-- Format --
File:
  - header
  - sounds

header:
  - "SSFF"              u32     0wx53534646
  - sampleRate          u32
  - sampleFormat        u16
  - bytesPerSample      u16
  - numOfSounds         u32

sounds:
  - sound 1
  - sound 2
    ...
  - sound numOfSounds

sound:
  - numOfSamples        u32
  - sample 1
  - sample 2
    ...
  - sample N

--- Library ---
It defines funcitions to write and to read a SSF file, making those usefull in a simple way.
